About Me

Albert Hong is an aspiring web developer/designer with a background in Human Resource Management residing in Edison, NJ. He is a recent Rutgers Coding Bootcamp graduate seeking to apply the learning from the program and continue to grow and mature within the field. He is an avid learner with a passion to create, analyze, and strategize. Currently while looking for opportunities, Albert is taking strides in honing his coding skills.


When Albert is not coding, he enjoys a diverse set of activies (not all at once, of course). He has been drawing at a young age and has a lifelong aspiration of creating his own graphic novel. He enjoys outdoor activites such as kayaking and hiking and loves to listen to a good tune. He might also consider himself a movie buff.
